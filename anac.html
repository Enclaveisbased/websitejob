ANAC – UAS Certification Internship

During my internship at ANAC, I worked on the analysis of the European UAS market and the aerodynamic prediction of failure trajectories for unmanned aircraft systems.

I developed mathematical models to predict vertical and horizontal cover after critical failures, using aerodynamic calculations to simulate descent behavior.

To support certification processes, I implemented interactive simulations with an HTML-based user interface, enabling accessible visualization of failure scenarios. These simulations represented an early step in the UAS certification workflow.
