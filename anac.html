UAS Failure Trajectory Prediction & Certification Support

During my internship at the Portuguese Civil Aviation Authority, I conducted analysis of the European UAS market and developed aerodynamic models predicting UAS behaviour after critical failures. Using aerodynamic equations of motion, I simulated: • Engine failure descent trajectories • Glide performance variations • Horizontal and vertical coverage envelopes I implemented these simulations through an HTML-based interface, allowing non-technical users to input UAS parameters and visualize predicted trajectories. These simulations supported early-stage UAS certification risk assessment processes.
