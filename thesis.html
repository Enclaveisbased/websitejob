Master Thesis – Advanced Propulsion Systems

Thesis work focused on advanced propulsion system design with emphasis on turbomachinery performance optimization, aerodynamic efficiency improvements, and integration within broader aircraft systems architecture. Research includes: • Compressor and turbine performance modelling • Thermodynamic cycle analysis • Structural considerations in high-speed rotating machinery • System-level integration impacts Further technical documentation and publications will be made available upon completion.
