Aerotéc – BLUE Micro Turbojet Development

As a member of the design team of BLUE (student propulsion project), I am responsible for the radial compressor subsystem — specifically the radial impeller and diffuser geometry. Work involved dimensionalizing turbomachinery components using MATLAB, modeling thermodynamic and aerodynamic behaviour, and defining design parameters including pressure ratios, blade angles, mass flow rates and efficiency constraints. CAD design was carried out integrating: • Aerodynamic performance requirements • Systems engineering constraints • Manufacturing feasibility This work directly contributes to the development of a small-scale turbojet engine for experimental propulsion applications.
